Hindsight

live in the moment, capture the past

Hindsight is a next generation pair of glasses which allow you to capture moments before and after they happen. Never forget something again.

when to use it

At a concert

Keep your phone in your pocket, with Hindsight you can record any highlights whenever they happen. Enjoy that sweet music and still make your friends jealous.

While you're driving

You're driving home after a long day and someone sideswipes you on the 405. You pull over and they start blaming you. You're not worried though. You used Hindsight after the fact to record an HD video of them hitting you.

Anytime

Whenever your Hindsight glasses are on, you're able to record something after it happens. It's up to you to decide what to save.

how to use it

The glasses

When they're on, tap the side of the glasses to save the last 20 seconds and the next 20 seconds. The videos you save will automatically sync with your phone when they're connected.

The app

Use the app to view your saved videos. From here you can share them wherever you want.

That's it

Really. We designed these glasses to be extremely easy to use.
